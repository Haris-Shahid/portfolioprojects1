Equity Business PowerPoint Template
TYPE : Powerpoint Template
   
AUTHOR :  Logann
Template 36991 is a Powerpoint Template website template. Purchase and download our PowerPoint Templates to present important information attractively and clearly. Everything you need to make a presentation is right here. Choose from a range of presentation designs - Marketing Plan, Business Plan, Idea Generation, Strategic Action Plan, Project Keynotes, and more. Our PowerPoint Templates are impressive and further your specific business goals while saving you precious time. With these ready-made presentations, all you need to do is change the text. You can choose to keep or change the images within the format provided. Our PowerPoint Templates come with a preview of the entire professionally-designed presentation, showing screenshots, graphics, images and text in an attractive layout. Our templates set out clearly where you can place your logo and text. The fonts are already specified in the design. Insert your own text instead of the sample text and you’re ready to go! In case you wish to modify the backgrounds and other design elements, the .PSD source files are also included (Adobe Photoshop is required for this). Microsoft PowerPoint 2003 or later is required for editing PowerPoint Themes themselves.
